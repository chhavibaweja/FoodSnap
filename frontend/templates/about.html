About FoodSnap

FoodSnap is a smart nutrition analyzer that helps you understand your food better. Simply enter a food name, and it instantly tells you the calories, protein, carbs, and fat!

This project is designed to promote mindful eating and make nutrition tracking simple, fun, and accessible to everyone.

Features

  • Instant food nutrition details
  • Modern, responsive design
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • AI-powered food analysis
  • Food search history tracking
  • Delete individual history items
  • Clear entire search history
  • Local storage support to save user history

Built With

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flask (Python), Google Gemini API and Browser Local Storage

What FoodSnap Does

FoodSnap allows users to quickly check the nutritional value of different foods and keeps a record of previously searched items so users can easily review or manage their past searches.
